How to send Self Compose Mesage For Tracking

Please note you must activate your account before trying this option.If you want to know how to activate your account then pls try this link (link)

Go To your Mail Box and compose new email message with To Field : tracking@mycvtracker.com

Subject : Any Subject of your Choice

In the Body Section You need to add this information to add tracking for traget as given in the example below

[RECRUITER_NAME]John Hunter [/RECRUITER_NAME]

[RECRUITER_EMAIL]john@hunterjobs.com [/RECRUITER_EMAIL]

[AGENCY]hunter jobs[/AGENCY]

Your Text that you want to send to the recruiter

You can attach your CV if you want to otherwise send email

Send Email



After few seconds you should get email in your account with tracking enabled in your message and attachment

You can either reply or forward this message to your target recruiter.

You will get email from the system whenever this recruiter is going to read your email.
