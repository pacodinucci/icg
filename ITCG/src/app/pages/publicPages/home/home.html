What To Expect

There is a great opportunity in the Information Technology market at the moment. IT Contractors are currently some of the highest paid professionals in the UK. Being an IT Contractor has a great deal of reward associated with it, yet it also has a number of challenges that must be faced and overcome before you can find the success that you desire. This group is about discussing mainly IT Contracting Topics and learn from other members different ways of managing your contracting career , how to keep up to date with upcoming HMRC tax regulations,get a new contract through our network members.

→ Want to become IT Contractor from your current Permie Role
→ Junior Dev looking for mentoring from senior guys in the group
→ Network with other IT Contractors
→ Compete in today’s business world & Keep up with technology
→ Workshops to help you understand how technology works.
→ Interesting presentations explaining agile and other ideas.
→ A lot of good cheer to meet other like-minded people.


Our Principles

IT Contractors Group is all you need to have a great and productive day. Focus on practice, We spiced it with some open space discussions and a networking lunch. We'll be discussing:

→ Fundamentals of programming
→ Tools and best practices.
→ How to apply this knowledge to make your projects successful.
